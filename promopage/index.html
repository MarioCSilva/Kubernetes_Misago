Welcome to misago - a Fully Featured Internet Forum

The main goal of this product is to provide an open-source application, easily customizable and integrable, where users can discuss and follow topics of interest.

What we offer

Topics

Create categories together with unlimited number and depth of subcategories.

Admin Permissions

Admin has moderation and ban permissions.

Thread Management

Users have in depth permission and moderation settings for their threads.

Notifications

Follow threads or users and notifies new posts.

Interactive Threads

Allows to create pools, attach images and GIFs.

Q&A Threads

Mark post in question thread as best answer, bringing basic Q&A functionality.
